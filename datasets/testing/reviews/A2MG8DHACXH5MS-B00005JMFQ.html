An interplay on the connections between eight different couples in the weeks leading up to Christmas. What a comedy of errors in London. The new Prime Minister has a new admirer on the No. 10 Downing Street staff who makes him jealous when the American President hits on her. A luckless errand boy flies to Wisconsin to catch American girls with his accent. Two body double actors meet on the set of a film. One lonely writer runs off to Portugal to get over a break up only to fall for his young house manager. An American advertising executive in love with a colleague. Her boss and his attempts to keep an attractive secretary at bay. A man secretly in love with his best friend's new wife. A school kid learning the drums to impress a girl before she moves back to New York. It's a British comedy on the craziness of affairs of the heart. But it works!
