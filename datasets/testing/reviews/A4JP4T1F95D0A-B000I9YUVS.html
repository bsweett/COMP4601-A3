This would have been 1 star save for a few nice special effects. I can't think of a single thing they improved upon from the original. Most everything is much worse. The story has been altered and most of the bite has been taken out. The director goes for a few jump scenes, rather than making the entire movie creepy ala the Carpenter version. The original ending was so good and dark. This one ends with a whimper that makes no sense. As for this being unrated, it is still a PG-13 cut. No nudity, little swearing, and very little blood. Stay away.
