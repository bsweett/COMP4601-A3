Nic Cage "stars" alongside his hair - bronze medal for most ridiculous movie hair behind David Spade as Joe Dirt and...Nic Cage in Con Air - Jessica Biel and Julianne Moore in a movie that was better the first time when it was called Déjà vu, and the third time when it was called Source Code. Essentially it's another Groundhog Day paradox movie, but in the future, where someone alters the current timeline through some sort of time travel mumbo jumbo. That sci-fi vehicle is driven by the FBI's desperate hope to stop random European terrorists from detonating a nuke in LA. As if anyone would notice. Agent Ferris (Moore) leads the effort by pursuing Frank Cadillac (Cage), a cheesy Las Vegas magician who just happens to have the ability to see two minutes into the future.

The filming has some really slick scenes, and the manner in which the filmmakers utilize Cadillac's talent is truly creative. Nothing worse than a lack of imagination. Right, Green Lantern? Adding in Jessica Biel surely doesn't hurt.

Fairly unoriginal, but aside from inconsistencies that are inherent in all sci-fi movies like this one, everything is done very well.
