Okay, the plot makes no sense and there are huge gaping holes in the story and it is so fast and furious it defies logic. BUT, it does entertain. What the hell? This is supposed to be Impossible Missions time and J.J. Abrams, Tom Cruise and company hit the deck running and barely let up to take a breath and throw in a little plot exposition.

Opening with a very clever flash-forward that lodges the hook and we're off as Ethan Hunt, out of field ops., is about to marry a civilian unaware of his IMF life. But a mission to rescue a former trainee of his gets him and the audience into not only a thickening plot but also into high gear for what becomes one smartly directed, choreographed, and edited action scene after another. The pace is unrelenting.

This is action movie stuff to the IIIrd degree if I may say so, and it is done with real movie smarts and a healthy budget. Cast and crew did their jobs well. It crackles and I was able to ignore the obvious leaps of logic and sit back and enjoy a rip-roarer done with style. I had fun, but then I like good action flicks. If you don't, you shouldn't go.
