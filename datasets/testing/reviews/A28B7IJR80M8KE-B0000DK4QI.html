Like "Alien" before it this film has a seven crew team that is hunted & eliminated, Arnold is great here as a leader of highly trained special forces unit, that get caught up in all kinds of mishaps (CIA double-crossings,unseen stalker) They are constantly watched by this unseen enemy, Jesse Ventura gives a memorable performance, Sonny Landham (48HRS.) gives a good performance as a tracker who senses the Predator & Carl Weathers gives a good performance as Arnold's advesary, but the best part in this film has to be the showdown between Arnold & the Predator, that starts off like a wresting match, the only movie that has Arnold getting his a** kicked, this was also directed by John McTiernan, a year before "Die Hard" , definately a man's movie, & probably one of Arnold's better movies.
