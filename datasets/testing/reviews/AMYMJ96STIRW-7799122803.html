It is not possible for me to read the reviews of others regarding movies like this without wondering how jaded they are on the marvels of visual art as displayed by masterpieces as this. These movies are lovingly and painstaking put together piece by brilliantly crafted piece with results...particularly like "Alice..." that are mind boggling. And yet all the nit picking!
I was overwhelmed by "Avatar" and then along comes this Johnny Depp starring plunge into the surreal.
What a joy to behold.
What a pleasure just to relish in the sheer ingenuity and the aesthetic rush.
This would be the movie to take to that "desert island" ... at least for now!
