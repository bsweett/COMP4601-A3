Boy, this was an amazing film!!! I mean, there are no words to describe it!!! The story, the acting was all great. The plot is a very simple one. A disgraced journalist is asked to look into a disappearance of woman, which took place over 40 years ago. Which seems simple enough, until strange things start to happen. Then journalist must call on the aide of an even more mysterious female computer hacker to help him piece together the mystery. This is one impressive film to check out!!! You won't be disappointed!!!
