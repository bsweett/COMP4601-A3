Lisa Reisert (Rachel McAdams) is the manager at the Lux Atlantic Hotel. After her grandmother's funeral, she takes the delayed red eye flight to Miami. After befriending Jackson Rippner (Cillian Murphy), he tells her his real reason for being on board. Jackson plans to kill the assistant secretary of Homeland Security, who's scheduled to stay at the hotel Lisa is the manager of. Jackson tells Lisa to call the hotel and switch the room that the deputy is staying at. He asks this so that the missile has a better target when it bombs the room.

This movie is full of action, and it will leave you at the edge of your seat the entire time.
