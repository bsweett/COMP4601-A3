Will Smith is a date doctor, who helps desperate men woo the women of their dreams to get them to fall in love with them. He helps his latest client, an accountant, make the women of his dreams fall in love with him. However while he is helping him, Will Smith gets in touch with a journalist and fall for her, but his techniques do not work on her. Eventually in the movie, he realizes that the best way to get women to like you is to simply be yourself.

Whats nice and new about this movie is all the practical tips given on how to impress a women. Things that you might not have ever thought about before and after this movie you realize, oh that makes sense.

The movie is fine, with few funny scenes. Not hilarious enough though!
