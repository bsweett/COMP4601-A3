Finally, after several overrated psychological dramas posing as superhero films ("Spider-man 1 & 2, "The Hulk," to name a few) comes "Fantastic Four," a thrilling film that is superior to most recent superhero films, second only to "Batman Begins." While the storytelling is a bit off, the terrific cast, epic action, and fantastic visual effects more than make up for it! If you're a fan of the comic book, you'll enjoy the movie. If you're new to the Fantastic Four, as I was, then this is a great primer! Prepare for the fantastic!

Grade: A-
