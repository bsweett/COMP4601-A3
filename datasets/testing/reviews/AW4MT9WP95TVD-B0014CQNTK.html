I really enjoyed this film. I thought the screeenplay was so witty and well conceived. (no pun intended). To the reviewer who said nobody talks like this: WRONG. There really are some smart, witty people out there with quick wits and good vocabularies.

Performances are uniformly great. Page is wonderful and Bateman continues to impress as he ages. Garner really surprised me with her brittle, edgy performance.

A good catch.
