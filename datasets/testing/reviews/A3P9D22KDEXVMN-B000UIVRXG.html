Hot Fuzz is very funny, with some excellent action scenes. Simon Pegg is just hilarious, and he has a great chemistry with the other actors, especially Nick Frost. The English humor - or should I say humour - is fast and furious in this movie. The Sleepy Hollow of a town and its excellent scenery are an unusual location for the fast moving criminal activity that follows. Not for kids with its R rating, there is plenty of violence and off color jokes. A 4-star thrill ride with laugh after laugh. Enjoy!
