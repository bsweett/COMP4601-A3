Comme Ci, Comme Ca.

Anne Hathaway casually out-quirkied Johnny Depp, who was mostly retreading and reusing old mannerisms, and Crispin Glover, who was handicapped by his costume. Mia Wasikowska held her own as Alice against the heavyweight stars. Visually; the movie looked good but didn't break any new ground. I was actually most impressed by the slowly growing mushrooms and vines framing the end credits. Lovely.
