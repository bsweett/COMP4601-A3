2 discs, the first with the movie (one of the greatest sci-fi movies ever with a great sound track), the second with tons of extras, including outtakes, featurettes on character design, stunts, special effects, interviews, makeup, location, camouflage tests of the predator and a photo gallery. Only flaw is the director commentary for the first disc - seems to be nothing but stuff he said during interviews spliced together. Kind of boring but not boring enough to take away a star.

Buy it new or used.
