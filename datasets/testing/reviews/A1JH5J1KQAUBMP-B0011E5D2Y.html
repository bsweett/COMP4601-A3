John and Jeremy (Owen Wilson and Vince Vaughn), masters at the art of crashing wedding receptions to hook up with young women aroused by the prospect of matrimony, find themselves at odds with each other when John falls for the daughter of the Secretary of the Treasury (Rachel McAdams) and browbeats Jeremy into supporting him in his canpaign to win her heart. Despite the great premise, there is not much that is fresh about this comedy, but it succeeds nevertheless because of the great chemistry between Wilson and Vaughn as well as terrific support from Christopher Walken, a surprisingly hilarious Jane Seymour (no Dr. Quinn Medicine Woman here), and a riotous cameo by Will Ferrel. The cast works so well together that even the obligatory phony dramatics at the end can be forgiven.
