I agree with a reviewer below - the addition of Dustin Hoffman and Barbra Streisand made this movie incredibly funny.

The first movie was OK, but kept mining the "surveillance" vein until it kind of stopped being funny. Here, having Dustin and Barbra for DeNiro to play off is a master stroke.

I also like Ben Stiller quite a bit. I think that he has a very expressive face and really has fun with what he's doing.

Must admit, though, that I thought Stiller and Meara were funnier. Does that betray my age??
