This is a well casted film with a interesting plotline- a presidential security agent is accused of treason and while he is hunted he has to find the real mole. However the film never really takes off as promised. So why does it earn three stars? As I wrote above, the cast is quite good and it is watchable so long that you do not have high expectations. Eva Longoria shines as a beautiful rookie and Kiefer Sutherland plays a role that mirrors his tv character is 24.
