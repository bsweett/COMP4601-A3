If you look at any of my other reviews, you'll see that I'm no "love story, chick flick wus." So there's my disclaimer. And there's your disclaimer. You can say: "some guy [(me)] on Amazon said, 'buy this flick; it's cool; it's not a chick flick; it's awesome entertainment for us macho, cool dudes.'"

Hitch is better than a lot of the "trying-to-be-macho-but-really-a-chick-flick" films that I've rented or purchased in the past several years.

Funny. Serious. Amusing. Witty. And, hey, I think I'll try some of the pick-up lines that Will Smith executes so flawlessly in this movie. HEY! OUCH! (That was my wife smacking me upside the head.)

Overview: Will Smith teaches guys "that need help" how to get the girl of their dreams. Will finds his own "challenge", and the comedy of Will and his protégé acquiring their respective women makes for an awesomely realistic, modern day (macho!) love story.

This flick is a keeper. Do you like movies in the vein of "Shallow Hal"? Then this is a movie for you.

Hit the Add To Cart Button...if you're macho.
