Captain America saves America (of course) AND this movie! Chris Evans makes Captain America a down-to-earth, refreshingly humble superhero. He starts as a scary skinny recruit, but becomes a super soldier. To his chagrin, he is used for patriotic propaganda. He fights back, and fights, FOR REAL. Tommy Lee Jones stars as his mentor. Dominic Cooper of The History Boys again stars in a period piece, though this time it's as Captain America's sidekick rather than a British schoolboy. Hugo Weaving, who was the gentle&brave elf lord Elrond (The Lord of the Rings: The Motion Picture Trilogy (Special Extended Edition)), relishes being the villainous head of HYDRA, Red Skull. When he removes his face a la Face/Off it's a truly creepy moment, showing how Red Skull has lost his humanity in his quest for power. Samuel L. Jackson also makes an appearance in the twist ending, making it like Unbreakable (Two-Disc Vista Series). "Captain America: First Avenger" goes for an over-the-top retro look, an exaggerated WWII era.

This would be an otherwise routine action film with explosions&gunfights, if it weren't for the humility and hunkiness of Chris Evans. He's likeable as Captain America. "Captain America" is all for the American way! Long live the USA!
