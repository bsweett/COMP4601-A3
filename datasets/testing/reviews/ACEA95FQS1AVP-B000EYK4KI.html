I agree with the other reviewers that the studios are just screwing the fans when they release a "special" edition less than 6 months after a previous bare bones release, which most of us bought. At least New Line had some decency when they let fans know there would be two editions of LOTR.

However, I don't want potential movie buyers to get the wrong impression based on the starred reviews. THIS WAS AN EXCELLENT FILM. A good bit of tongue in cheek humor, action and explosions, and Pitt and Jolie sizzle on screen together. If you don't already own this--get it now with all the extras.
