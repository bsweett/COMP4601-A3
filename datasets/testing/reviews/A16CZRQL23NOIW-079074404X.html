"Forbidden planet" was probably one of the most boldest sci-fi projects whose only default was to have been extremely challenging for its age. The underneath message about a pretended form of outer energy understood as the projection of our own interiors demons lacked dramatic intensity due the secondary plot ( you should be notice this issue would be carved in relief in Solaris thirteen years after). But in case you make to take the risk you are still on time to enjoy (although certain outdated special effects) an interesting proposal to watch and discuss.

A referential cult movie since its appearance!
