In many many franchises & trilogies, the 2nd movie is the weakest in the series. Fortunately for us, this isn't the case here.

The most noticeable thing (well, except for the boys' voice changing) was the fact that the younger actors' acting has improved tremendously (esp. Daniel Radcliffe, while Rupert Grint can use a little more help...). The adaptation of the story was much better this time, too.
