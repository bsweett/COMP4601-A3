"Salt" is satisfying if you like treadmill action and spies coming out of the sewers. It has action and thrills, with an outrageous plot that keeps popping surprises. Most of the crazy double crosses you can guess, but it is still fun to watch. Angelina Jolie plays the Russian Mole CIA Agent, Evelyn Salt, with gusto. She keeps your interest with fast-paced escapes, clever disguises and turn on a dime attack scenes.

The plot revolves around Russian sleeper agents coming to bring down the United States. The agents are brain-washed as orphaned children who are sent to adoptive families in the United States. As they mature and reach responsible positions throughout the country, all come to life as evil agents with specific plans to bring down the USA. Evelyn Salt is the only hope we have - oh my!
