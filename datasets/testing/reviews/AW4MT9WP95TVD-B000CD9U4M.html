This is a wildy visual and funny ride from Tim Burton. I love Burton films and I love Depp, so I may be just a tad predjudiced, but I had a smile on my face from begining to end. Depp's Wonka is innocent bordering on malevolent. He had me in hysterics. Burton's best work wonders with sets and effects providing a visually beautiful and magical world. You can still like the original, but it's okay to appreciate this vivid retelling too.
