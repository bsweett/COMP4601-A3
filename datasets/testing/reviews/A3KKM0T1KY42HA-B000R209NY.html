To the preservationists who say that one should only re-make bad movies...not good ones...think this: One should re-make old movies and make them at least as good or better. Poseidon accomplishes neither.

The Poseidon Adventure (1972) was a better movie than Poseidon. It had better actors. And sadly the special effects were just as good in the original from over 30 years ago. Shameful.

Here are some examples of random silliness without the movie trying to be silly:

-Lots of fires but absolutely no smoke.
-Civilians are smart enough to try to find a way out of impending doom but none of the ship's officers agree.
-A guy blindly dives from a ledge into a flooded room assuming that it's deep enough and there will be nothing in the water to impale him.
-Several rafts conveniently waiting upon escape from the ship into the water.
-Immediately after escape the ship sinks...nick of time escape is goofy.
-Rescue calvary consists of 2 commuter-type helicopters...not the coast guard.

Oh and there's plenty more silliness. Don't buy this. Don't rent it. Wait for cable at best.
