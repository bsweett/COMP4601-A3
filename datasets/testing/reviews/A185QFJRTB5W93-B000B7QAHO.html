This film did not need to be made. I guess that is my main problem with it. I wanted to enjoy it, because I loved the classic, but this is a far cry from that. The original was a dark comedy...this film just made everything too right at the end.

The beginning was also way too drawn out. Come on, we all know that Charlie eventually gets a golden ticket...let's get on with the story already.

Depp had me laughing at a couple parts and the obsessive sports-mom had a few good lines, but otherwise I was unimpressed. How about originality out of Hollywood? Wow, does every film need to be remade? Can't you people think of anything new?
