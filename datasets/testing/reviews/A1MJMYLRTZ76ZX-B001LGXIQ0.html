From all the negative reviews of this movie, you'd think the title was "Get Dumb!" But as a long time fan of the original, I enjoyed it. I thought the movie stayed close enough to the original concept and that the actors were all good enough so that the movie was an overall enjoyable flick.

Ann Hathaway and Steve Carrell aren't quite as wacky as the originals, but then who is? Don Adams had a quality of goofy exaperatedness which is hard to imitate, but Carrell is still good in his own inimitable way. I thought they both did a decent job with their roles. Terrence Stamp, Bill Murray, and Alan Arkin, were all good as I would expect, and The Rock was better than I expected and in some ways stole the show.

The movie reprises many gags from the original, such as the cone of silence, the shoe/radio, the line "Missed it by that much!" and few others, and I thought those came off well too. Overall, I thought this was, although perhaps not great, still a good reprisal of the original show for an old, somewhat persnickety fan of the original series.
