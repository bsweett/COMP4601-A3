This movie has plenty of action, stunts, and special effects, and the acting is generally pretty good, so it's reasonably entertaining. However, the characters and plot stretch believability past the breaking point, with the result that I didn't find this movie very engaging and it left little impression on me when it was over. I suppose I can recommend this movie to people who are in the mood for it, but there are definitely better movies available in this genre, so this isn't a top choice (despite having Angelina Jolie in the lead role).
