Very sweet movie, one of my favorite Arnold action classics. Everyone of the main 8 characters does a good job of acting (even Sonny Landham who I recently learned was a porn star). The special effects will still stand up today against modern action pictures. The movie quality is pretty good, but I don't really have any good sound systems to test it with. The documentary on disc 2 is a little sub-par of what I would expect. I'd rather see something more in depth like on the quadrilogy discs, those are documentaries. The deleted scene + outtakes are pretty interesting along with the test footage and other featurettes. What I find most puzzeling is that there isn't a single trailer for this movie, just a quadrilogy trailer. Everyone should see this movie, and if you like it, than this DVD is great for you. If you don't like the movie I have pitty on you.
