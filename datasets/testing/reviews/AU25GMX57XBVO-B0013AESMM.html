I am not a big Jodie Foster fan but I have to say this movie I really liked, her role & the movie. It has the 3 things I look for in a movie; keep my attention from beginning to end, take something away from the movie or can personally relate to it and a good, appropriate ending. I don't want to spoil it for people who have not seen it, but the ending was great. Sometimes an ending in my opinion can make or break a film. Try this movie out, think you will like it.

Peter J. Miller
