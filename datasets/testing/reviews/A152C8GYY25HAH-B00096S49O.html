I generally don't like formula movies. "Hitch" is a comedy based on a formula going back decades and decades - boy gets girl, boy loses girl, boy gets girl. Yawn! But Will Smith is such a great actor that he's able to take a stale script and turn this movie into something quite enjoyable. Add to his performance a great comedic turn by Kevin James, and you've got one very predictable but very funny movie.

Smith plays Hitch, A New York City matchmaker. Gauging from his lifestyle, he's a success. His latest client is a big, goofy guy [James], who's secretly in love with a beautiful heiress. Hitch grooms the guy in all the right moves. Meanwhile, Hitch meets a girl [Eva Mendes] who will turn his life upside down.

The plot is absurd. It's the performances that make this movie work. Smith and James make it something both touching and loud out loud funny.
