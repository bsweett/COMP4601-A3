The first film that overtly had to do with the remembrances of a secret service agent trying for keeping alive the life of a President was "In the line of fire".

Nevertheless, this movie experiences a sensible twist of fate, when precisely there's a love affair between the President's wife and one of the best agents (Michael Douglas), who likes to seduce wives of married men. As a matter of fact he was involved into a painful incident with a colleague ten years ago (Kiefer Sutherland). But soon the things will change for hi, when a sinister plan in order to kill the President begins to appear.

The rhythmic vitality of the film, the unbearable dramatic tension, the stunning cast, the formidable direction, the splendid photograph and the solid edition, make of this thriller a must-see.

A winner!
