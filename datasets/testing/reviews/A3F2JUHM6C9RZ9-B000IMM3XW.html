It's the future, and an evil genius named Magneto controls the fate of mankind. The only ones standing in his way are the X-MEN, a superhuman group of wayward transexuals in black spandex catsuits who rail against the messengers of doom and injustice. A rollercoaster ride from start to finish, X-MEN is the perfect panacea for a world ripe with bigotry and intolerance. A must-see.
