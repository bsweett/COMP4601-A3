I really liked this movie. I had heard reviews from people that loved it, and some who hated it, so I watched this one expecting something decent, but not great. This is a very un-PC movie. To many it seems crude and vulgar, but it is much more than that. A lot of the people who dislike this movie don't get things like South Park, which are over the top, but are really terrific satires. Borat is a funny movie. Sacha deserves the riches he made from this movie just for the nude wrestling scene alone. I would never have done that in a million years. I would rent this one if you are not sure if it is to your tastes. For anyone else, this is a must buy.
