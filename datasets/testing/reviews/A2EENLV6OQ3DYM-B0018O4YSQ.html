"Dark City" is the intellectual sibling of the action oriented "The Matrix." The film deals with similar subject matter (just what IS reality, anyway?) and has an even gloomier visual style than "The Matrix." There the similarity ends as "Dark City" explores the darkest depths of the imagination. The visuals are stunning, the acting is superb (biggest revelation, Kiefer Sutherlan CAN act) and the ending is quite satisfying. This is a sci-fi film for those who spend their time reading serious sci-fi novels rather than "Star Wars" serials.
