Cleverly editted film using tongue-in-cheek humor in a candid camera/jackass kind of way. The plot is simple, and the parody of Kazakhstan makes for a cliched start. However, the best parts of the film are Borat's hilarious adventures in America. Cohen must have picked up a few pointers from his psychologist brother because it has definitely served him well with this film. Clearly a hoot and eye opener on real Americana. The jokes on us, folks!

Definitely worth a watch despite the naked-wrestling scene.
