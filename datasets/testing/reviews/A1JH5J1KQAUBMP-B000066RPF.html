This film was my introduction to Monty Python. When I was a boy, the local PBS station aired it frequently. I was hooked instantly and began watching the television series as well. The Pythons pioneered a delightfully absurd brand of comedy that has been imitated but never equaled. I actually enjoy some of their later films more (particularly "The Meaning of Life"), but this skewering of the Arthurian legend remains one of my all-time favorite comedies.
