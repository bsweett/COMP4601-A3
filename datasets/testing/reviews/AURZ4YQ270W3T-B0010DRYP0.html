Ever wonder why people get into the Mob to begin with? Watch this film and find out.

It's a seductive lifestyle, but as Henry Hill (Ray Liotta) finds out, it's a life that requires you to screw your friends, destroy your marriage, and live a lie.

And by the end of the film, even after Henry is stuck in a nowheresville subdivision in the witness protection program after losing it all, he STILL wants the mob life back.

It's the power of amorality, brought into crystal clear focus by Martin Scorsese.

Brilliant film, full of marvelous performances and wonderful moments.
