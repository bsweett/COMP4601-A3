Many of the other reviewers have given the plot summary, I just will say that this is a decent action film. Great camera work and nice effects. The only thing I had to that was a flaw was the fact that indeed, his friend was killed in Ethopia on a top secret mission when they were left for dead. And yes it left his widow and family grieving. But where does this justify after getting caught up in a big cover up shooting and killing so many people. Does that justify the outcome. In this movie, no. What did it prove, a corrupt senetor, and an aged evil secret agent (danny glover). So, bottom line, don't overthink this one, just watch it for the great camera and effects work.
