Don't buy this movie if you're looking for a FACTUAL and HISTORICAL account of Amadeus' life... if you want to get to know the "real WOLFGANG A. MOZART, this movie is not the one for you, because there are glaring factual inaccuracies. But if you realize that going in -- as I did -- you can simply sit back and appreciate the well-written thoroughly-entertaining package this movie presents.

In my opinion, It's a must-see!
