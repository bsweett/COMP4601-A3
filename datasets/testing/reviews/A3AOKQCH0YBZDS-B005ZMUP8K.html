I am mainly giving this movie such a high rating for the following reasons:

1) Johnny Depp is perfectly cast as Captain Jack Sparrow in this movie and gives a phenomenal performance that no other actor could have given this role. Just the facial expressions and body language Depp employs for his role alone, shows his immense talent as a skilled actor.

2) The costuming, make-up, set design, lighting and cinematography are phenomenal for this movie and look very realistic..... especially for the character of Davy Jones and his crew.

3) Phenomenal computer generated scenes with the kraken/squid attacking the pirates ships at sea.... It is truly amazing how far computer generated scenes have come.

I think what was a deterrent for me in terms of watching this movie and why I didn't rate it as "5 stars" was because:

1) This movie's storyline dragged out way too long. I felt that a lot of the scenes were just used as fillers/fluff to stretch out the movie and make it longer..... Along with showing off all of the special effects that may be employed today. I think this movie would have made a lot more sense to the viewer in terms of the storyline, if it could have been made shorter.

2) This movie was reminiscent of the Jules Verne novel 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea.

3) Keira Knightley can't act. Sure she makes nice eye candy, but that is about it. I think that some other actress with better talent should have been cast in the role of Elizabeth Swann.
