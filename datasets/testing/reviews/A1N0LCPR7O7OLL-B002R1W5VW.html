Highly entertaining film has one of Nic Cage's best performances to date. Very well directed by Ridley Scott. Sam Rockwell gives an astoundingly good comedic performance and Alison Lohman gives a terrific performance as Cage's "daughter." The cinematography is so strong I can't even tell you. Look for Bruce Mcgill in a key supporting role. The soundtrack is to die for...Sinatra, Sinatra, Sinatra and one of Hans Zimmer's best scores. This is easily one of my favorite films of 2003.
