Yep, one of the best James Bonds ever has a major role as one of the Possible Dads in this musical masterpiece - and he does a great job on the songs, folks.

The inspired casting is just one of the things that makes this such a delightful experience, and I highly recommend the 2-disc DVD, for the invaluable insights into the production, cast interviews etc.

One of the most unbelievable technical achievements is the fact that most of the Greek village is actually a set, inside and out.

With a cast that includes Meryl Streep, Julie Walters and Colin Firth, the quality is a sure thing, and the screen adaptation by the trinity of women behind original breathtaking stage show is exceptional.

Loved it.
