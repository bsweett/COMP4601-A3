I thought that there was something wrong with this movie. Johnny Cash appears one-dimensional, and the story kept jumping from one incident to another. If they kept a bit of continuity, (like showing how he gained acceptance in his field rather than cut from him making his first record in a country store to being a spectacularly famous singer with a spectacular following) it would more cogent and more enjoyable.

I remember Johnny and June Cash as people with more substance than this movie shows. The movie was a poor attempt to capitalize on the memories of these two performers who are still in the minds of much of their public.
