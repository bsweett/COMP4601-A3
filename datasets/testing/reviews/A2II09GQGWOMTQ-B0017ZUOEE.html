This movie was recommended to me by my Amazon friend Ron.
Ron seems to know my taste in movies and TV pretty well by now as "The Orphanage" was right up my alley.
Definitely a horror movie but if you're expecting "Saw" or "Hostel" stop reading now.
I'm a fan of slow burn horror that actually requires you to think.
Gore doesn't frighten me.
Mind trips are much more effective and difficult to come by nowadays.

The good?
Excellent acting, direction, score, pacing....pretty much everything was done perfectly.
I LOVED the ending, so........never mind I don't want to spoil it.

The bad?
Nothing really.
I heard they're doing an American remake....oh joy (deliberate sarcasm).
The movie is in Spanish so subtitles are something you can tolerate or not.
Other than that.......nothing.

An incredible movie that deserves to be seen by the (me included) jaded American horror audience.
Highly recommended.
