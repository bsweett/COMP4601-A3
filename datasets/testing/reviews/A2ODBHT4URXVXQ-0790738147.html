I keep getting this confused, when people are talking about it, with Chinatown - and no wonder. The similarities are striking, but the biggest is that both movies are terrific.
LA Confidential is a gritty tale of scandal, betrayal, corruption, and that essential ingredient: sex.
Incredibly well-acted roles by a host of actors: Kim Basinger, Russell Crowe, Buy Pearce, the inimitable Kevin Spacey, Danny DeVito - and that only scratches the surface.
Plenty of reviews go on and on about this movie - and for good reason. However, I'd like to avoid being repetitions and will just add my vote to the others:
GO RENT IT. Now.
