Mathayus the Akkadian (Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson)defeats a powerful warlord (Steven Brand) and rescues a beautiful sorceress (Kelly Hu) in this Conan-lite adventure. The Rock showed that he had the presence to carry a film, but the story is ridden with cliches and the narrative is strewn with coincidence. There is always a handy escape route when the Rock needs it, and he always encounters unexpected comrades when they are required. Brand is miscast as the warlord; he is not physically imposing and never quite seems like a match for Johnson. The only things that raise this film (barely) to the 3-star level are the Rock's charisma and some decent action scenes.
