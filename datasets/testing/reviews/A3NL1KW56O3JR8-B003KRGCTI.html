...but not bad either. Like always, Washington is awesome but the plot leaves more than a little bit to be desired. The underlying idea was kind of cool but the execution is somewhat strained. It's a little slow (especially in the beginning) and has a little bit of a "Mad Max" feel to it. Still, it's worth watching.

Recommended.

As an aside: how the hell do they still have gasoline 30 years after the collapse of civilization? They have no refining capacity (as far as I can tell) and, if I'm not mistaken (I'm not a chemist), gasoline degrades after a year or two and becomes unusable...

Not important to the story but, still, that just bothered me...
