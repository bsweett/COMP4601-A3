Please describe the audio and video quality of any Blu-Ray you review.

The video of this Blu-Ray is fantastic. I didn't see any grain at all. In fact, even when paused, the video is perfect, and without grain. I give it 10 out of 10. This movie must have been recorded digitally, instead of on film. The audio is great also (Dolby TrueHD 5.1), but don't expect a lot of surround sound, since most of the movie is dialog. The surround speakers do kick in when appropriate.
