Now, changing his identity, he must find out why and by whom his wife was murdered. It's a tense thriller that keeps you rooting for harrison ford all the way. He's just too good to let go. A detective is getting closer to him and wants to track him down . . .but aling the way he gets the feeling that maybe this man was framed. Intrigue to the point of proud acheivement is what this movie offers. You'll love the quick dodges, the chase scenes, and the clues to finding the true killer.
