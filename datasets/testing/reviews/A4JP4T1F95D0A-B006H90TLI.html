This was a 5 star movie the first half. Things didn't run as perfectly the second half, but it is still an outstanding picture. The desolation and bleakness is overwhelming at times, as is the extreme violence that explodes on the screen. Based on the Cormac Mccarthy novel, this is a wonderful cinematic experience. Great acting from top to bottom, this is a film I will buy the day it is released on dvd. Though it is dark, there are a couple of quirky scenes which make you laugh. The only problem I see for people is the ending. This isn't a typical hollywood ending. All of the loose ends are not tied up and everyone goes home feeling good. I am looking forward to listening to the commentary while watching the movie to get a better insight on some scenes. This is a must buy.
