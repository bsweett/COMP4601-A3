This film has a lot of filler that needs to hit the cutting room floor. The tale unfolds initially disjointed, but the pieces do fall in place after a long,long time. This is a good story about a very cold, stoic man who has a love for his country, but constrains himself so much that he looses sight of his family and personal life. There is a lot of crossing and double crossing, which is complicated, but drug out longer than it needs. Overall, worth a viewing, and a nice change of pace for Angelina Jolee and Matt Damon, but not worth buying.
