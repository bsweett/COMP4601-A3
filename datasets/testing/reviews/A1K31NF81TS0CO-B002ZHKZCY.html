I was thoroughly disappointed with this one. I expected better. I saw the original movie, with Matt Damon as Bourne. I remember it being much more interesting. But this movie is so choppy and lacking in explanation. I couldn't follow the story for the life of me. The interaction and tension was a little high, and good, but mainly droll and boring at most times.

I honestly didn't have a problem with the camera work. I know that it helped me to believe that the characters were moving quickly. And this was the effect that the director and special effects team wanted. It worked for me.

I had a problem with the dialogue, and the interaction between the characters. That was my main problem.

I give this three stars as it wasn't the worst I've seen. It was a'iiight.
