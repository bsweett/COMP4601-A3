The newly-remade Mr. & Mrs. Smith is quite awesome, both comedically and in an action/adventure sense.

This was much better than I even anticipated. It is quite obvious why Brad and Angelina are together in real life. Their chemistry here is astounding. Both are at the top of their acting game here. From bad-ass assassins to romping sexuallly through sheer force of willpower and muscle - both are shown with aplomb in this film.

Whoever this director is, he is on the right road here. Great action cinematography, along with comedic timing, the director shoots all with pinashe.

A must see film.
