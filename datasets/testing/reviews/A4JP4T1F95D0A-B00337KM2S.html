My wife wanted to watch this film, so I rented it hoping for the best. I have to say that I enjoyed it a lot more than I expected. A romantic comedy/drama this film kept me entertained throughout. Clooney as charming as ever plays a corporate hatchet man who is traveling 240+ days a year and loves it. He is not close to his sisters and has no family of his own. It is a really interesting character study and the surrounding cast is very strong. Some parts are a little cheesey, but enough works that I can recommend the movie.
