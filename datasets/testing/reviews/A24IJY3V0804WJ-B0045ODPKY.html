Originally this movie was written for Tom Cruise but somehow got offered to Jolie but Jolie brings such energy to the role that it is difficult to imagine anyone else but her in this role. As Evelyn Salt, who is a CIA agent on the run after being accused of being a double agent for the Russians. Superb production values along with some very superb twists, turns, car chases and action make this fare palatable. Philip Noyce, the director has promised a sequel. Four stars. 2/25/11
