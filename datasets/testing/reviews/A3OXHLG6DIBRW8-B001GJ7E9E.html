"Mr. And Mrs. Smith" is an enjoyable and very watchable action flick that also has a lot very funny scenes interspersed through out. This review is for the "R" rated Amazon OnDemand version.

No plot spoilers from me: The movie centers on a typical upper-middle class couple living a seeming normal life in suburbia, but both leading double lives as assassins. The pace of the movie starts out a little slowly, but picks up as their relationship and work lives begin to collide.

There is definite on screen chemistry between Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie and that goes a long way to compensate for some of the slower scenes and occasionally hokey situations.

This is not a very deep movie, but it is a good choice when looking for a lot of action, a little romance, and a laugh or two.

Recommended!
