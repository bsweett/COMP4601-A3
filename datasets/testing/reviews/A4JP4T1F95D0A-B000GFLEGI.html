I enjoyed this movie, and I eagerly waited for it when I heard that Romero was getting another chance at the genre he created. The movie is good, but not all I would have hoped it could be. Since it has been so long between dead movies, I was hoping for more to the story. It certainly has promise, but doesn't feel completely fleshed out. If rumors are true, and this is part of a new trilogy, I may have to update my review. Right now, it didn't leave me completely satisfied.
