FINAL DESTINATION 3 begins at an amusement park, where another group is lined up for their (delayed) slaughter. This time, we get a horrific roller-coaster disaster, predicted by the lovely Mary Elizabeth Winstead. Of course, the real fun begins when Death starts reaping the souls of those who have "escaped" their untimely demise. FD3 works in the same way as the first two installments, by preying on our fear of death and the supernatural / unknown. I mean, surviving a catastrophe is supposed to be a "miracle", not a reason for further dread and terror! I get a kick out of these films and the way Death uses the machinery of everyday life in order to orchestrate our doom. The tanning-bed sequence is my favorite "accident" in this one! Enjoy...
