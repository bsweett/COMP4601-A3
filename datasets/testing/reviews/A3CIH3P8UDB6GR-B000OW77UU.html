I must say with the deepest warmth about this movie. I thought it was going to be corny. A real father and his son portraying in a movie about a father and son movie. Above all, Will Smith. Now, I remember him when Parents just didn't Understand. But he won me over easily. I felt his struggle. Just when I think he would come out of it, something else happens. It was like Keep kicking him when he's already down. I thought Will Smith did a great and inspiring job. His son was good, but Will Smith did an amazing job. Hats off!! Great motivation piece of film with a message of never give up, by all means.
