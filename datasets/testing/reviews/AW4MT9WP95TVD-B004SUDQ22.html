I have to admit, I was surprised by this one. I was convinced it would bore me to tears; but quite the contrary. The cast is uniformly excellent (surprised at no recognition for Davis and Paltrow at award time).

Anyway this unfolds like a mystery and surrounds itself with a hint of depressed madness. Dour sounding - but it's not. Gyllenhall brings great energy to his scenes and Hopkins is all demented gusto.

If you are looking for a thought-proking piece of drama, stop here.
