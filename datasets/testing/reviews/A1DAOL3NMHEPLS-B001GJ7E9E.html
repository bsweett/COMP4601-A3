"Mr. & Mrs. Smith" blends two genres -- high-speed action and bickering couple movie -- with fair success. Is it even remotely plausible? Of course not. Is it great cinema. Nope. Is it fun to watch, if you don't mind a lot of cartoonish violence? Yes. Brad and Angelina work well together, and the script has occasional flashes of wit, so if you don't mind suspending your disbelief a lot for two hours, this is passable entertainment.
