I didn't have much anticipation for this film and I though this was a routine chick flick. However, Will Smith shows that he can take any acting role given to him and make it work. He's become a veteran entertainer. This film surprised me. Kevin James (King of Queens) is hilarious as the accountant, trying to win the heart of his boss. Eva Mendes is a lovely presence on the screen as the gossip columnist who uncovers Hitch's secret. The story is wonderful, original and doesn't take itself too seriously. It's a refreshing change of pace and will entertain all who watch.
