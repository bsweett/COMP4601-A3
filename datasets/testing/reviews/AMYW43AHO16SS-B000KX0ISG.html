Quentin Tarantino makes his motion picture debut a memorable one. Tarantino is clearly a fan of the movies. The interraction between the characters and who these characters are makes his style different than any other filmmaker out there. The closest I would say he comes to immitating is Paul Schrader & Martin Scorsese. The acting is top notch. Harvey Keitel, Steve Buscemi, Michael Madsen and Tim Roth are all outstanding in their roles. The movie is raw, violent and unforgetable.
