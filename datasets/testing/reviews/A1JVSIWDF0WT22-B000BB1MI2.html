Having gone through his horrendous PLANET OF THE APES re-thinking / re-imagining / re-whatever, I had forgotten how good a film-maker Tim Burton can be. This is a pretty good film full of magical moments but marred by Johnny Depp's disastrous-Michael Jackson-like-performance.

Here's a tip to Tim Burton, the world is full of actors, next time at least consider the possibility that Johnny Depp might be miscast for a part.
