DVD Running Time: 2 hours, 30 min. Rated "PG-13".
The first movie, Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (Two-Disc Collector's Edition) (2003) is based upon the "Pirates of the Caribbean amusement ride at Disneyland, Anaheim, California.
Now this second movie, Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest (2006) continues the story of our pirate, Captain Jack Sparrow.

At Port Royal, it is William Turner and Elizabeth Swann's wedding day, however the ceremony is disturbed by rain and the presence of Cutler Beckett (Tom Hollander), who has arrived to serve a warrant of arrest to William (Orlando Bloom), Elizabeth (Keira Knightley) and James Norrington (Jack Davenport). The arrest is for helping to set free pirate, Jack Sparrow (Johnny Depp).
Meanwhile, Jack is in search of a key. This key will open the Dead Man's Chest of Davy Jones.
Mr. Beckett will give William and Elizabeth a full pardon if they bring Jack back to England. What Beckett really wants is Jack's broken compass.

Also in the cast: Naomie Harris, Bill Nighy, Stellan Skarsgard.

Shown in widescreen.
