PLOT SUMMARY:

After being given permission to marry the daughter of a retired CIA agent, Gaylord "Greg" Focker, his fiancee Pam, and her parents travel Miami, Florida to meet his quirky liberal parents, Roz and Bernie Focker. The film features a new character, Little Jack, who is Pam's nephew, and Jack and Dina's grandchild. Jack tries to nurture his namesake grandchild.
