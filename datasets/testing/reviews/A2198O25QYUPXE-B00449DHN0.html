"Gangs of New York" is a historical action drama film set in the mid-19th century.

The narrative captures a time in American History where the streets of New York were essentially ruled by criminal mobs, and the hostile environment of the time is brought to life with eye-catching set and costume design.

There are several themes presented by the story, which includes: calculated vengeance, displacement due to personal loss, and attempts to seek order in amongst the mayhem.

The movie doesn't hold back with fierceness, and this is used as a means to convey the harshness of the story events and time period.

Nicholas R.W. Henning - Australian Author
