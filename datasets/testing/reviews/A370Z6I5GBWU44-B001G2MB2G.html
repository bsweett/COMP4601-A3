Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix was one heck of a book, and the movie lives up to it. Special effects are used well in this movie, and the writing was done well. And good god, the actress who played Umbridge was spot-on, right down to the sugary-sweet voice, attire, and office decor! So many movies prove to be disappointing adaptations, but so far, the movies for the Harry Potter series continue to deliver.
