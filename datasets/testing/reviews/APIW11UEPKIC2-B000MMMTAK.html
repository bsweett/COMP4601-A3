You have probably seen 'Da Ali G Show' on HBO, you will know Borat as the idiot reporter from Kazakhstan. We actually get to see his town for the first time I believe and it was a real treat. Now the thing tha I really loved about thsi movie was that it didn't try to be a movie. It does try to make a couple of plot points but doesn't worry about them. It gives itself every oppurtunity possible to tell a joke. This guy will truly do ANYTHING for a laugh. Unlike the HBO show, a few parts do feel a bit staged. This is not a bad thing because I promise you that the finale is totally orinigal and I did not expect it one bit. And of course the nude fight scene cannot be missed. It is hard to look at but once you have seen it, it is impossible to look away.
