I enjoy movies, even ones that are WAY out there. And this one was. But I still liked it - escapism at its best.

Angelina Jolie is beautiful even when thin; I enjoy Liev Schreiber and Chiwetel Ejiofor. All three have great screen presence. The action sequences were exciting even if not very believable. I watched the theatrical version.

I enjoyed the plot and it kept me guessing (I figured out some of the plot twists but not all). I would watch a sequel of this movie.

My only small complaint - does anyone really think they can disguise Jolie when she has those lips sticking out in front of her?
