Today I saw this with my two little cousins. I have to admit, I liked it.

The story is pretty simple. Five people get superpowers after being exposed to a space storm. One of them becomes the evil Dr. Doom. The become the FANTASTIC FOUR!

I enjoyed this flick. I hope there is a sequel. Filled with fine special effects, cool action, and some humor, FF is a must see summer movie. My favorite has to be the Thing. Don't listen to the "offical" Ebert and Roeper reviews "Fantastic Four isn't so fantastic! Hardy-har!" Screw 'em.
