Great cast and great direction. The problem is that I figured out the big twist 10 minutes into a 2+ hour film. I had hoped that maybe it was a big swerve and the ending would change things up since it seemed so obvious, but that didn't happen. I do like Scorsese's direction and the score reminded me of "The Shining". I can't fault much with the film other than the story itself. Well made movie, just a bit of a let down for me.
