Recently I bought the blu-ray of this film. I had forgotten what a really excellent movie it is -- dramatically as well as visually. I can see now how it deserved to win the academy award for best picture. I noticed that it was photographed using Panavision equipment. I certainly hope the Panavision corporation is able to resolve its current financial difficulties. I would hate to see the loss of a corporation that contributed so much to the technical excellence of motion pictures.
