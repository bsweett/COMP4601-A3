Lisa is certainly having a bad day. Returning from her grandmother's funeral she meets a man who seems quite likeable, but is really a villian. Lisa works as a manager at a hotel in Miami where the deputy secretary of homeland security and his family are taking a room. Our villian tells Lisa she is to help him put this man and his family in a position to be killed or her father will die. What would you do?

At first the movie is slow going as the plot is laid, but once this fiesty woman decides to fight back hold onto your hat.

She's stuck between a rock and a hard place but is determined not to be the loser. I felt this movie had a great story, really good acting and I loved the way it ended. A giggle after the suspense was icing on the cake. I think you'll like this one and recommend you give it a try.
