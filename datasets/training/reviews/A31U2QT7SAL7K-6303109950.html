Pair this one with Clint Eastwood's "Unforgiven" and you have the two superlative movies of the last decade. This remake far surpasses the original "Gunfight at the O.K. Corral," with its perfect casting of Kurt Russell, Powers Boothe, Sam Eliott, Bill Paxton, and Jason Priestly who shows that he can exist outside of the "90210" zip code.

But it is Val Kilmer, one of Tinseltown's most gifted "chameleons" who immerses himself as the legendary Doc Holliday. Everyone in my circle of friends lauded his performance as one of the finest that has ever graced the silver screen.

The guy can act!
