I like Will Smith, and I read Asimov's original "I, Robot" long ago, as a teenager. So I had high hopes for this movie, but they were not fulfilled. The production is pretty good, and the film's basic message is OK -- but the high-speed action sequences aren't even remotely believable, as Will Smith fends off computer-generated robot armies time and again. Not really a bad movie, but it could have been so much better!
