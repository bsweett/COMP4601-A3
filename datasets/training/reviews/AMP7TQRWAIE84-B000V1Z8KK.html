The plot was boring, the characters were stale and the dialogue was nothing spectacular. It even had me snoring before the end. I had to pop it back in to watch this disaster piece from the beginning.

Mark Wahlberg was hot as usual, but next time he should read the script before jumping into a movie such as this one!

Not recommended and should be avoided!

Rent something more exciting and relate-able, Perfect Stranger for the former and American Beauty for the latter.

2 stars.
