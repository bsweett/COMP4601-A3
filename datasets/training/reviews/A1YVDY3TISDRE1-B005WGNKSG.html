don't want to give away too much of the plot, but...let me just briefly say that if you like thriller/suspense type of movies, then you will definitely want to see this.

movie is based on a Scandinavian book, which is a world-wide best-seller. In fact, there is a trilogy of books, and movies based on those books, of which this film is the first in a series of three.

The plot involves an investigative journalist; he is hired by a man to investigate the death of a niece of his dating back decades ago. There are possible clues that the person was killed by someone in her own family.

Along the way the journalist finds help and assistance from an unlikely source and they develop a close bond.

* this is the best thriller movie I have seen in years. Can't wait to watch the other two movies in the series.
