Well, I finally got to see this one and I am kicking myself that it took me so long to get around to it. What a wonderful film! It is so creative and romantic and beautifully acted that I was riveted to the screen. Carrey - who usually makes me really crabby - is just great here. I can't even describe how this film finally changed my opion of him. Winslet is like a piece of of eternal sunshine, Dunst, Ruffalo, et al. are each excellent. This movie is funny, moving, suspenseful and romantic all in one. Beautifully filmed and with a great score, I can't recommended this one enough.
