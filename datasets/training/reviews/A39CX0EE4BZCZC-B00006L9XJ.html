I like westerns and I like this film but I admit this is a bit hard-edged. It took me a couple of viewings to appreciate it. It's a tough movie with a good cast and no nonsense. The characters in here aren't the talk-now-shoot-later variety. There are a few memorable and very tense scenes in this film, and a good share of profanity, too.

Watching the likes of Clint Eastwood, Gene Hackman and Morgan Freeman as the three main stars, however, is pretty entertaining. How often are those charismatic actors NOT entertaining?

Overall, a rough film but a pretty solid western which would have been probably better and accepted by the public better had the language been toned down.
