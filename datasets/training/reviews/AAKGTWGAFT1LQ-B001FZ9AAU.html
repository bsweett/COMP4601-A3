I'm very glad that Robert Downey, Jr got his act together.
It is largely because he did that we have this film. He holds it on his shoulders ably.
Like other comic book based movies, I don't care about the source material at all.
The only question : Is the movie entertaining - yes it is, in fact is for that reason that I give it 5 stars.

This movie is a master class in acting from Robert - whether that be comic or dramatic.
What other lead actor could play Tony Stark? Granted, that is an easy question, as there are not that many real 'actors' out there at the moment.
The reason I say this is that Robert has to play an unsympathetic yet occasionally charming and intelligent man.
This in itself is a challenge - which he rises to admirably.
People have to work with him, but they don't have to like him - in fact, that does lead to problems for the character.
Of course the supporting cast are right there with him - at or near his ability level.

The last line of the film is delivered perfectly by Robert and leads in to the next movie.
The last line is perfect because everything previous to it leads there logically - it is the only possible culmination in the film's world. You are left hanging on with a sense of anticipation, yet you laugh as well.

Perhaps one day we will see a Director's Cut of this, though I won't be surprised if we don't, as it is perfect.

If I could add one thing to this Blu-Ray set, it would be a commentary by the director and stars.
Very highly recommended.
