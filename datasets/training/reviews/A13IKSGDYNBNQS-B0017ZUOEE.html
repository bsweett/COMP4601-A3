I'd be interested to know whether this is an original story written for the film, or a screenplay based on a book. Either way its a high-class ghost story with more tension and suspense in it than I have seen in a film for many years. Stand-out amongst a very good cast is Belen Rueda in the leading role of Laura. Her performance as a former child resident of the orphanage who has now returned to own the property with her husband is superb.

The nearest recent film you could compare this to is The Others, however unlike that film I felt much more involved with the characters in this film, and it will certainly keep you interested for its full 105 minutes as well as maintaining a level of intrique throughout its length as well.

What this isn't is yet another torture-porn movie with the requisite number teenagers being hacked into small pieces. Neither is it a film with exploding cars and endless gunfights. Don't misunderstand me theres a place for those films, and I've watched many of them, but I'm grateful for something different every so often.

This is different and for me gets 4.5 stars.
