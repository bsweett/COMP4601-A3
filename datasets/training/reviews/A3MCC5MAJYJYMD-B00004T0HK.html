Although certain moments are powerful and moving, other moments are very ridiculous.
The basketball game with the whites vs. blacks is embarassing and so is the scene where Edward Nortan tries to stuff meat down his own sister's throat. It is manipulative filmmaking; the filmmaker wants to scare the audience with the evil main character.

He is no longer the genocidal skinhead, but is an open-minded good guy who wants to save his brother from the skinhead groups. All of this happens so abruptly it is unconvincing. Let me tell you the real reason why Ed Norton's character changed; he did not want to go back to prisom where he would be gang raped.
