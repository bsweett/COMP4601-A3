"Wedding Crashers" is excellent! Owen Wilson and Vince Vaughn make an excellent comic team! The directing by David Dobkin (who directed Wilson in "Shanghai Knights" (2003), Vaughn in "Clay Pigeons" (1998) is excellent! The story & screenplay by Steve Faber & Bob Fisher is excellent! The music by Rolfe Kent (who also did the music to "The Matador" (2005) & "Just Like Heaven" (2005) is excellent! The cinematography by Julio Macat is excellent! The film editing by Mark Livolsi (who also film edited "Elizabethtown" (2005) is excellent! The casting by Lisa Beach & Sarah Katzman (who did the casting for "Walk The Line" (2005) is excellent! The production design by Barry Robison (who also did the production design to "Fun With Dick And Jane" (2005) is excellent! The art direction by Kevin Constant (who did the art direction to the upcoming "Glory Road" (2006) is excellent! The set decoration by Garrett Lewis (who also did the set decoration to "Fun With Dick And Jane" (2005) is excellent! The costume design by Denise Wingate is excellent! This is an excellent and funny film!
