By far the best Cohen film ever, and that's saying a lot. This movie is glorious. The Javier Bardem character is fantastic; his cool efficiency is very impressive. I particularly like the gas station scene - great interaction with manager guy. The dialog throughout is very good, the tempo is great, sense of dread, etc. Note, though, this is a good degree of a cerebral film, not your standard killer fare. Lots of mood setting, slowly building events, etc. Brilliant though.
