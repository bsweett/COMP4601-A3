Based on the 70s exploitation flicks that had a stupid plot and enough sex and violence if you weren't laughing at the stupid plot.
There's a lot of known actors and actresses in this picture so they're all in on the joke. That said, you have to like these types of films and I have mixed feelings about it. Didn't really grow up with this stuff so it's a bit weird at times, though, I did laugh at the intestinal scene. DIALOGUE/CHARACTERS: C plus to B minus; VIOLENCE/SEX: B; STORY/PLOTTING: B minus; OVERALL GRADE: B minus; WHEN WATCHED: Mid March 2011.
