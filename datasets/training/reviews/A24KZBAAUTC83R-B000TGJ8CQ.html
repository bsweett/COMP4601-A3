NEXT had all the ingredients of a good idea, but was poorly executed by it's screenwriters and its actors. I really liked this concept, but was quickly bored to death after about 30 minutes.

When you find yourself yawning and fast-forwarding your DVD, that's not a good thing.

Plus, the bomb that this was at the Box Office should have raised a big red flag. But despite that, sometimes we moviegoers don't find all the hidden gems until later in movies.

This ain't one of them.
