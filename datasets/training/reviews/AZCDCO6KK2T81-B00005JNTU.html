A great summer film with really special special effects, a fun cast, and a simple story that is well-paced. This is fun for the whole family. I especially like that it isn't burdened down with Fruedian psycho-babble like so many comic book movies. That has it's place and can add to a film, but this superhero story would just be dragged down by all that. This is just fun popcorn entertainment. A pleasant diversion from anything serious.

The direction is swift and competent and doesn't get in the way. Each hero/actor pulls his or her own weight and they all work well in this ensemble piece much like the X-Men series characters do. The script is fun and funny, but nothing special. It is a paint-by-numbers storyline. The music is unremarkable for a superhero film and that is a serious flaw by my standards.

This film has great repeat value and perfect for a day when one is shut in. It is well-worth owning.
