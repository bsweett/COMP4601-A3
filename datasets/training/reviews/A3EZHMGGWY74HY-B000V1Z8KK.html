"They" can say that Marky can't act, but "they" are wrong. His skills have improved greatly over the years and I think any poor presentation on his part was due to poor script/storyline. This one kept us on the edge of our seats...Rambo and Bourne Identity all wrapped up in one. The only thing I thought the film could do without was perhaps the cliche scene of our lovely "love interest" holding a shotgun while wearing her finest Victoria Secret apparel. It just seemed to predictable that we would witness her in her skivies holding a weapon...sometimes these movies really are all the same. But, all in all, an exciting ride that you won't want to miss.
