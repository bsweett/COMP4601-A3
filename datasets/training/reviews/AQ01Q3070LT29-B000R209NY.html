This 2006 film is a remake of the original 1972 film, The Poseidon Adventure (Special Edition) (1972) released December 12, 1972. That was followed by a sequel, Beyond the Poseidon Adventure (1979). Both films included an all-star cast.
When Warner Brothers Pictures announced their remake of "The Poseidon Adventure", NBC/Hallmark quickly put in to motion their version of the Possidon disaster, titled The Poseidon Adventure (2005 TV Movie) (Full Screen Edition) (2005-TV), starring Steve Guttenberg. It was broadcast on NBC, November 20, 2005.
Thus, the new motion picture version by Warner Brothers Pictures was titled, POSEIDON (2006)", released May 12 2006.
So, yes, two Poseidon Adventure films were being made at the same time. One as a television movie, the other for the big movie theatre screen.
This film continues the tradition with an all-star cast: Kurt Russell, Richard Dreyfuss, Josh Lucas, Kevin Dillon, Emmy Rossum, Jacinda Barrett, Freddy Rodriguez, Kirk B.R. Woller, Gabriel Jarret, Gordon Thomson.

The Captain (Andre Braugher) senes something is wrong about the ship. Meanwhile, the New Year's Eve parties have been in full swing. Exactly one minute after Midnite, the Captain sees a tidal wave headed directly for the Poseidon. As the giant wave of ocean water hits, the ship capsizes over, upside-down. many people die. Those that survived will have to find a way back to the top. However, they are standing on what was the ceiling. The floor is way up there. They better hurry because the below zero freezing water is about to rush in.

None of the actors in the original 1972 film make an appearance.
