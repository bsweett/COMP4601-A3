It appears this might be eastwood's final staring role in a movie and what a great way to end a long successful career. Gran Torino has a little of everything to satisfy most movie buffs. A well written story line with some action, suspense, humor and even maybe a few tears for the soft hearted. I guess the film touches me personally since I was born and from the Detroit area but brings awareness the issues of gang's in many big metropolitan areas of the country. As a lone solider, eastwood takes on these gangs with his own brand of justice.

Peter J. Miller
