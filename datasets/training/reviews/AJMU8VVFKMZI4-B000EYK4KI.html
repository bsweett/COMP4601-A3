Never has an actor and actress have more chemistry and Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie. Their chemistry and an entertaining script give readers more than just eye candy. I haven't enjoyed a Brad Pitt movie in years-might be as far back as 'Seven'. Going in to see this film, I wasn't expecting funny. This movie is indeed that.
The movie starts off with two assasins attending marriage couseling. (That in itself is a hoot) and quickly escalates to bond action freezy when the husband and wife are ordered to off each other. Any more than that will totally be giving the story away--but this movie is a definite must-see.
