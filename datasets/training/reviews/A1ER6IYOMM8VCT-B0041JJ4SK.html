With a title like this you don't expect too much, but Harold and Kumar's first adventure is surprisingly fun! The two leads have good chemistry in this thread bare plot about two buddies who take a trip to eat burgers and wackiness ensues!

Sure it's raunchy at times with some nudity, but that shouldn't bother this film's fan base. It's a fun trip with some amusing cameos along the way. The DVD has two versions of the film and I will add to this review after watching the unrated version.
