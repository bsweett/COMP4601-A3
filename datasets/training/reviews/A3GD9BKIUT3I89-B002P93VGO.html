I went into this one with low expectations but walked out glad I spent the time. Entertaining, basically a good summer popcorn film. Sure you have to suspend your disbelief at the door -- but really, any movie of this type requires that. A better film than X-MEN 3. True, it doesn't have Famke Janssen but there is something equally beautiful, the very alluring Lynn Collins. If you like comic movies a good one to have.
