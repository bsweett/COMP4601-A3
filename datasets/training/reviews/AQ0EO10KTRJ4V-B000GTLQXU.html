Ugghhh.... 40 minutes into this remake disaster, we hit the EJECT button.

Yes... with modern effects the waves were bigger, the flames bigger the explosions were bigger.....

So what was missing ?

SOUL and a concern about the characters.

Unlike the original with Gene Hackman, Ernest Borgnine, etc...
