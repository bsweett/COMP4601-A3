Ok, ok. Here we go. 24 is a.......what the heck did I just watch! Oh, The Sentinel. Okay. Sorry it just reminded me of all those movies(which are better bye the way). I was thinking of seeing this in theatres, thank goodness I didn't. Its not really a bad movie, its just seems like its been done so many times before. It was kind've like a special 1 and a half hour episode of 24, with a cool helicopter blowing up added in. I'm just happy that Harrison Ford didn't join up on this project, Firewall was misguided, BUT I enjoyed that more than this, of course Ford is one of my favorite actors so I'm biased a bit.

The acting was good, like I said before it was a bit like a made for TV movie. It would be great to watch it on the SCI-FI channel or FOX, BUT for a movie that came out in theatres it was bland.

Overall only rent this if you want to see Michael Douglas or are a huge 24 fan. I, personally would rather watch Jack Bauer for an hour than this, but thats just me.....
