The lead actors sing with their own voices in a very convincing fashion. Reese Witherspoon brings June Carter to the silver screen opposite Pheonix's Johnny Cash. In a true story of a love that begins as a childhood crush (little Johnny listens to June on the radio before meeting her years later as an adult) and later a pining that last several years, June and Johnny take may roads before finding their way back to one another. It's a relationship fraught with Cash's addictions, marriage, inner demons, family fueding and Carter's own turmoil, career highs and lows, and years of refusing to acknowledge her own feelings for Cash.

The most telling scene is their duet on The Johnny Cash show that reflects Cash's despondency that Carter will never be his:
