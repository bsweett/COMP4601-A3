For those that like the film-like quality of their prints re-calibrated and compromised to look "cleaner" for smaller high-definition TVs (under 50 inches), then this new, extras-added, version of PREDATOR will be satisfactory.
The original Blu-Ray, without the extras, looks much more like the theatrical release prints, so purists should hang on to that version.
This new version has the rather lackluster extras, and questionable internet options, that adolescents who watch movies in cars and on computers probably crave, but if you project PREDATOR on a large (65 inches and over) home-theater screen, then there's no need to spend any more money on a newer version.
