Lights...Camera..ACTION!!! That is what you will get with this great film, and lots of it!!! This is one of those films that you should expect lots of violence...lots of cursing...but the story is great!! A bit unbelievable in some areas, but overall, very good. This movie actually had an ending that I was satisfied with!! Lots of movies today, just leave the viewer hanging...say what??? Not this one...action, twists and turns...surprisingly, this is a film most any action lover will enjoy!!
