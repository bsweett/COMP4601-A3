I found little meaning in this movie. I enjoyed the idea and the historical setting, yet it seemed a little surreal.

It was much like 'Once Upon a Time in America.' If you like that type of movie, you would like this.

The senseless gore of the film was what irked me the most. DiCaprio is not too believable, yet Lewis rocks as a the archetypical villain. He is utterly believable.

It is a story of revenge. DiCaprio's character at least did not give up his quest to avenge the death of his father.
