Carpenter must be very hard up for money as that would have to be the only reason he would ever let someone remake his classic. Like a number of his films, the original The Fog failed to find an audience until being released on VHS/DVD where it is now considered something of a classic. It had one of the best atmospheric direction jobs I can ever recall. How could anyone think they could improve upon this film is a mystery to me. Steer clear of this fog and go buy or rent Carpenter's original.
