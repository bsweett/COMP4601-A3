One wonders if the rodeo audience, for just one example, realized how ridiculous they were and how much they deserved the skewering that they didn't even know was satirical! Is this film rude, crude, gross and outrageous? Yes, guilty on all counts. But that's what makes it so effective. Cohen exposes the dark, hypocritical side of the U.S. by being the earnestly obnoxious Borat.
