I am not a big fan of "Meet the Parents." I wasn't looking foward to a sequel until I heard the casting of Dustin Hoffman and Barbra Streisand. I knew that these two additions would be a happy addition to the mix and compliment the rest of the cast.

The sequel itself is funnier and fresher for some reason. Its not just a one joke movie, it's more developed. Hoffman and Streisand are fabulous. Babs has never been funnier, and I wouldn't be suprised if she gets an oscar nomination. Hoffman is equally great. The real scene stealer is a baby. He steals EVERY scene he's in. Also the pets steal one scene. Lotsa laughs abound in this movie. It is one of the best comedies of the year, and I was pleasantly suprised of how good it was.
