I was definitely skeptical when I heard of Chris Evans' casting for this role but he more than made up for it. I'm not a big Marvel fan ... or Captain America fan, for that matter. I consider him to be just a big propaganda creation for the U.S. military. And while he is just that, this was still a really well-done film. It was not as good as X-Men: First Class but it is right up there with Iron Man (Single-Disc Edition).
