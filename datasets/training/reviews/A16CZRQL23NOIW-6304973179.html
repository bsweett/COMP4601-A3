Predator has achieved though all these years a cult status, due this intriguing, absorbing, bloody but extremely effective sci fi. To fight against an elusive who invisibly displaces from wrench to wrench and is capable to detect (as the snakes do) the human being through a formidable thermal sensor, will convey the viewer to a very engaging portrait of devastation in the middle of the jungle.

The final confrontation may be regarded as one of the most extraordinary ones in the history of cinema.
