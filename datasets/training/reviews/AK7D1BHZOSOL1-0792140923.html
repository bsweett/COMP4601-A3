Forrest is basically a good guy, he just happens to be kinda slow. But that doesn't seem to stop him from meeting Elvis, fighting in Vietnam, Investing an apple and founding a multimillion dollar shrip company.

Tom Hanks is brilliant and he plays the part wonderfully. Although some elements of the book that I would have liked to see were not included it still does not take away from a wonderful proformance. This movie will have you laughing and crying at the same time.
