After finding the Half Blood Prince to just be dark and dull, I wasn't looking forward Deathly Hallows: Part 1 much at all. With that said though, I was pleasantly surprised how well done the movie was.
The film isn't just dark with barely any charm or laughs like the last film. There's plenty of magic, excitement and heart-felt moments that will keep you entertained through-out. This Potter is easily one of the years best and one of the best Potters!
