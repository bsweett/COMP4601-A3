Geez, Meryl Streep is phenomenal in her role as the editor-in chief to elite fashion magazine "Runway" and plays the perfect boss from hell. I can't imagine any other actress for this role! Also nice to see actress Anne Hathaway mature as an actress and move beyond "The Princess Diaries".

This movie is fast paced, humorous, and a total chick flick at heart. I loved every second of this movie.... Sure its no Oscar Nominee, its not meant to be anything other than just pure entertainment.
