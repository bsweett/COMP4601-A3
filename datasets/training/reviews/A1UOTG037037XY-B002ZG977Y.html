But the mistakes in camera closeups and the mumbling of Liam Neeson and Ralph Fiennes are irritating. Also, wearing a beard while mumbling as both these actors do give the effect of chewing on dryer lint. I need closed captioning to follow this film so I can't imagine this was a pleasant movie theater experience.
