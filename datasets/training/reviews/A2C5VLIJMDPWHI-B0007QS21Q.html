Robots are as common as humans in this movie, with better accuracy and less violence (Fed Ex deliverers to cooks), but the Three Laws go out of control when one robot is given experimental power over the rest and a suicide is reported. Will Smith plays the detective to link this confusion together.
I had never thought about seeing this movie because I'm not into science fiction, but I do like Will Smith. This movie had some clever lines, interesting characters, and even the robots were entertaining. I loved seeing the futuristic cars (with no gas, I wonder what their running prices are?), trains, gadgets, etc. The only problem I found with this movie is that Will Smith should've died a half hour into the movie but somehow kept living through train crashes, robot attacks, water, etc. And what ever happened to the cat in the trunk?
