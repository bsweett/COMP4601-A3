Hitch...what a man!! When you are down on your luck and can;t seem to get a date...Hitch is the man for you!! :) Will Smith plays a guy who consults people on their dating habits and who best fits what they are looking for!! Kevin James (King and Queens) fame, plays the average dork who is looking for love, but cannot find a way to best meet people. The two hook up and snare this lovely lady and the rest is history...find out what happens in HITCH!! Cute, Funny...and just downright Enjoyable!!
