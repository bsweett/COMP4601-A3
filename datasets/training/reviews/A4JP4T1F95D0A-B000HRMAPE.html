This is a summer big budget action film. It is certainly more than your average Arnold picture in the way of plot, but at the core it is a popcorn movie. Though there are twists and turns, this one is less confusing than the first film. Much of the technology is improbable, but you have to suspend a little belief. I mean, yes people can make lifelike masks, and if given time can make a person resemble another. But there is no machine that can make an exact copy in less than a minute. Cruise is good in his usual charming way. Hoffman is great, and I would have loved to see more of him. The movie is a worthy addition to the franchise, and it is a shame it did not do better. I would certainly like to see a 4th.
