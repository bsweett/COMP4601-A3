I've loved Martin Scorsese ever since watching BOXCAR BERTHA in the theatre as a kid. I've especially enjoyed his gangster films (GOODFELLAS, CASINO, DEPARTED), also TAXI DRIVER, RAGING BULL, and CAPE FEAR. However, deep down, I'm a horror / thriller fanatic, so SHUTTER ISLAND came as a wonderful surprise! From the outset, the entire atmosphere is gloomy and full of dread. The characters are interesting, and the story is dark. Leonardo DiCaprio is perfectly suited for the twists, turns, and shocks involved. If you love suspense, mystery, and thrills, then SHUTTER ISLAND should flip your lid!...
