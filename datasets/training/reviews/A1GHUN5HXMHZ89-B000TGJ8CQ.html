This type story has been visited many times, but this is a nice variation on the theme. I didn't feel I was watching a remake of anything. Cage plays a magician with a Vegas act that gets noticed by a government agent played by Moore. She wants him to find a bomb terrorists have planted. Now it sounds predictable and it is to some degree. However; I was never bored or felt the story was out of line. Overall a reasonably entertaining SciFi movie. I recommend renting before buying. I think it is replayable.
