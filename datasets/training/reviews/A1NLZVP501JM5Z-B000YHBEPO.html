Mission Impossible 3 is the perfect summer movie. Things get blown up, its got a great villain(Phillip Seymour Hoffman is excellent as one of the biggest jerks in a movie since Denethor in the Return of the King) and its got a great twist at the end I did not see coming! Tom Cruise is Tom Cruise and the other actors are pretty good. I am going to buy this when it comes out. It gets summer off well, very well.
