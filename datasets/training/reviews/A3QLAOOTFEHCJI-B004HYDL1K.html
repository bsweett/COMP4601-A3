Director Robert Rodriguez takes another stab and what has become a successful run of "grindhouse" films. This move was initially shown as a trailer between "Death Proof" and "Planet Terror" when they appeared as a double feature a few years ago. This is a brilliant idea as Rodriguez finds "A" list actors who elevate the style of the movie. Granted this is stupid, dark and over-the-top violent but the movie doesn't take itself too seriously. I had a lot of fun and certainly enjoyed seeing Lindsay Lohan in new light!
