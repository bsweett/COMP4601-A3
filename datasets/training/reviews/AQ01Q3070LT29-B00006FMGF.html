Returning: Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint, Emma Watson, Richard Griffiths, Fiona Shaw, Harry Melling, James Phelps, Oliver Phelps, Julie Walters, Robbie Coltrane, Alan Rickman, Richard Harris, Maggie Smith, Bonnie Wright, Chris Rankin, Tom Felton, Ben Borowiecki, Harry Taylor, Matthew Lewis, Devon Murray, David Bradley, Jamie Waylett, Josh Herdman, Alfie Enough, Eleanor Columbus, Sean Biggerstaff, Emily Date, Danielle Tabor, Warwick Davis, Luke Youngblood, Scot Fearn, David Holmes, Oliver Lavery-Farag, Amy Pugliam, Geraldine Somerville, Adrian Rawlins.

Harry Potter (Daniel Radcliffe), now 12 today, looks through his photo album to see his mom (Geraldine Somerville) and dad (Adrian Rawlins) and the friends he misses at Hogwart's School. He is still living with the Dursley's and his Aunt (Fiona Shaw), Uncle (Richard Griffiths) and Cousin (Harry Melling) are still mean to him. At least he has his own room upstairs now. His Uncle Vernon wants him to be very quiet while they have company and no magic stuff. Harry receives not a very pleasant visit from Dolby the Elf (voice by Toby Jones). He causes problems for Harry and his uncle puts rails on his windows. He will never allow Harry to go back to the Hogwart's School.
Later that night, Harry receives a visit from Ron Weasley (Rupert Grint) and his brothers Fred (James Phelps) and George (Oliver Phelps) in a flying blue Ford Anglias. They are there to rescue him and his white owl.
In the Weasley's house, Molly (Julie Walters) shows Harry how to get back to Diagon Alley.
Back at the school, Harry deals with the Chamber of Secrets and destroy its horror.

Also in the cast: Jason Isaacs, Kenneth Branagh, Miriam Margoyles, Shirley Henderson, Christian Coulson.
Julian Glover as the voice of"Aragog". Leslie Phillips as the voice of "The Sorting Hat".

This was the last time Richard Harri would play "Professor Dumbledore". He passed away October 25, 2002 at the age of 72, before the release of this movie. He had Hodgkin's Lymphoma.
