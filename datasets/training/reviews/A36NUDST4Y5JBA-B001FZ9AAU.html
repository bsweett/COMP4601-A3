As a kid, I bought a few Iron Man comics (which my Dad would dismiss as junk and throw in the trash...but I digress). Action a'plenty and I even liked the romantic tease between Paltrow and Downey. The Burger King tie-in just cracked me up (What? No McDonalds?). Were liberties taken with the story? Absoulutely! Still an enjoyable film. Four stars!
