What I liked about this story was how dazzlingly original the characters and plot elements were. A young farmboy who finds a secret weapon and becomes the savior of the land? A bearded wizard who guides him to maturity? A spunky princess in need of rescuing? A besieged group of good guys who triumph unexpectedly when all seems lost? The henchman's evil right-hand man who is killed, beyond all hope, at the very last moment?

Truly groundbreaking.
