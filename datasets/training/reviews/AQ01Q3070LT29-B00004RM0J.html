The entire younger cast of The Goonies are reunited on this DVD version in the Special Features/Commentary With Hidden Treasures. On camera is Jonathan Ke Quan (Key Huy Quan), Corey Feldman, Sean Astin, Director Richard Donner, Kerri Green, Martha Plimpton, Jeff Cohen and Josh Brolin.
The reunion and commentary is fun to hear and see. Also in the Special Features section are three outtakes, the original theatrical trailer, a short documentary "The Making Of The Goonies", and the Cyndi Lauper music video "The Goonies 'R' Good Enough" Part One and Part Two. The first outtake is 3 minutes long. The second and third outtakes are about 2 minutes long. Watch out for that giant octopus!
I enjoyed the reunion of the cast.
