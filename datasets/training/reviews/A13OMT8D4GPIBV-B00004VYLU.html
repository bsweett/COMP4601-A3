The argument for the best mob movie ever can made endlessly. The Godfather DVD Collection, Scarface (Platinum Edition), Goodfellas, Casino, even Miller's Crossing gets mentions. But Goodfellas is my favorite movie and the best ever to accurately depict the mob at it's best.

This is the tale of a rise to power for one man and it all being snatched away from him. One of the best movies of all time.
