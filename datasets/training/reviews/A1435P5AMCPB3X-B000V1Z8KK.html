This movie is non stop thriller, starting out with action, and ending with action. There are a lot of twists in this one. Mark Wallburg is a Sharpshooter who retires after a mission on which his spotter gets killed. Three years later he is pulled into consult on a possible assasination attempt on the president, and gets involved in a conspiracy of unimaginable proportions. Mark Wallburg has been an underated actor for years, but should be given more credit because he takes roles he fits well. This is a great suspense thriller/action film that you should take some time and watch. Worth a purchase also.
