I could list all the problems with Wedding Crashers, from its generally formulaic story, to its decent, but entirely unimaginative directing, to a script that doesn't give the stars a heck of lot to work with, and, finally, to a blasé ending. But, despite all of that, it has a number of geniunely funny moments, genuinely beautiful women of all stripes, and solid performances from Owen Wilson (who I generally like) and Vince Vaughan (who generally repulses me). The two work well together, albeit with a minimal amount of chemistry between them, and the supporting cast members, including the always entertaining Christopher Walken, are a nice treat. (Will Ferrell's cameo performance was incredibly lame. Or am I just getting sick of Ferrell's one-note schtick?)

This a fun rump. A guilty pleasure.
