You have an all star cast and director, but not quite an all star movie. This is an intelligent piece of work, very subtle and well acted, though the characters are very subdued. The problem is the pacing for me. At 2 hours and 40 minutes it seemed to drag for me. This isn't a Bourne movie with lots of action. Sure there is a kill here or there, but for the most part it is a character driven story. I enjoyed the movie, but I didn't find it compelling enough that I would purchase it for future viewings. I would say to rent this one first to make sure it is your type of movie.
