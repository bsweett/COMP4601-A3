Sometimes you probably wonder, like most people, if life is really worth living. If you are like many, this is often the case during the holidays. This classic film is a gentle balm for the holiday malaise. It is corney and sappy, its true. But the message is nonetheless true and will tug at your heart like few others. Its a must have/must see movie. The grandady of all "feel good" movies you will not want to miss.
