Nicholas Cage plays Roy, a quirky con man struggling with OCD, whose life is turned upside down by the sudden arrival of Angela (Alison Lohman), who claims to be his 14-year-old daughter that he didn't even know existed. When Angela takes an interest in her father's profession, Roy allows her to take part in a job that he's running with his parner (Sam Rockwell). The tables are quickly turned and it becomes unclear who's conning who in this fast-pace "dramedy" that will keep the audience guessing until the very end.
