This was an excellent followup to the "Bourne Identity".The

hero of the series,Jason Bourne,is set up to make it look like

he committed a double murder.He then becomes wanted dead or alive

by the CIA.An assassin tries to kill Bourne but misses.He thinks

it is the CIA and comes out of retirement.He is pursued by the

leader of the Task Force well played by Joan Allen.After he

follows up on leads he unravels a huge conspiracy involving some

members of the CIA as well as the Russian Mafia.His search for the truth takes him to Berlin and finally to Russia.In Russia

you will see a spectacular high speed chase.The movie ends leaving room for a sequel.This is an outstanding movie that you

will enjoy watching. Don't miss it.
