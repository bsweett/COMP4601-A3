The Bottom Line:

Helena Bonham Carter is wonderful as Bella Lestrange, but this movie adaptation of one of the richest (and best) books in the series feels condensed to the point of lunacy; the book's spectacular (and very cinematic) concluding chase through the ministry is almost entirely cut, whole months of the book seem to have been excluded, and I have no idea how someone unfamilar with the novel would keep up--why make the longest book into the shortest film?
