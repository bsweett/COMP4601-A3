Finally! I've never bought GOODFELLAS on DVD because a film this great deserved far more than just throwing the film on a disc to get it out there. I'm thrilled to see it on a 2-disc set.

The three great Mafia movies are THE GODFATHER, GODFATHER II and GOODFELLAS. They make up a trilogy that covers the mob from its beginnings in America up to the 1980's when, fulfilling Vito Corleone's prophecy, "this drug business will be the end of us."

Are there other good Mafia movies? Sure.

But these three are firing on every single piston.

Pure classics.
