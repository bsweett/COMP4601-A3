While the movie started off rather ridiculously fake with all sorts of unusual voice acting, it eventually got better. The only problem was the entire story wasn't interesting enough to care about, so after 50% of the film went by, I really lost interest. I also didn't care for the slow motion effects showing people dying and blood squirting from their bodies. The V character was decent, but he can't save an average film. Some of the special effects were good (such as that exploding clock) but that's about the only thing worth noting I found about the film.
