In this first movie that's the beginning of a series of Swedish films, famous reporter Mikael Blomkvist (Michael Nyqvist) teams up with hacker Lisbeth (Noomi Rapaca) to solve the case of a woman that disappeared 40 years ago.

We find out only a little about Lisbeth in this first film, for one she hates men for a good reason. For one, a slimy wealthy man gives her money in return for sexual favors and he's also abusive. So when she meets Mikael, she has trust issues. While he is fresh off of a divorce and has issues of his own. But how they bond is touching and intriguing and what makes this violent and even often disturbing film work so well. Since their relationship is moving and Mikael and Rapaca have great chemistry.
