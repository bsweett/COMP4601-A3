I can't get overly excited about this movie. The plot is no secret: victim-becomes-vigilante who not so slowly slides from self-defense to pre-meditated murder. Of course, the people she kills are all one-dimensional s**t-bags for whom the viewer has no sympathy so you're almost tempted to think that what she's doing is right.

The best thing I can say is that TBO avoids the cliché ending I was expecting; kudos for that.
