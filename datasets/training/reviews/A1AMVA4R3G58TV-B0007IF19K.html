Ray is an absolutely perfect movie. The script is extremely interesting, and tells the life story of Ray Charles. It keeps its pace, and you never loose interest. It shows you his life as a child as he was going blind - and his life as adult, with his band and his family.

Jamie Foxx is outstanding as he portrays Ray. He has the character of this famous musician down to perfection.

And the music is wonderful too. The soundtrack is fabulous, taking you back to the creation of some of the best music of the time period.
