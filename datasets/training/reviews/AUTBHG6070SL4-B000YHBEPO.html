Tom Cruise does a great job with the Rube Goldberg plots and gadgetry. There's a lot of running around on rooftops, swinging through the air, and other good fun. There was one huge cop-out though. We see Cruise enter the top-secret bioweapon lab (downtown Hong Kong? is that really the best place for it?), and leave a moment later with the goodies. C'mon guys - what happened inside? Armed guards? Secured labs? Guessing the way around an unfamiliar rabbit-warren of lab corridors? All in a day's work, I guess, nothing worth writing about.

It's good excitement, but don't expect anything more. If you have an evening to kill at the movies. Cruise is the man to kill it for you.
